Summary

The Dark Knight follows Batman as he faces his greatest psychological and moral challenge yet when the Joker emerges in Gotham City, creating chaos and forcing Batman to confront the line between heroism and vigilantism.

Actors

The movie stars Christian Bale as Bruce Wayne / Batman, Heath Ledger as the Joker, Aaron Eckhart as Harvey Dent, and Michael Caine as Alfred Pennyworth.

Favorite Quote

“You either die a hero, or you live long enough to see yourself become the villain.”
